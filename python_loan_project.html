Analyzing the Impact of a Government
on a financial platform

A data-driven simulation of a government micro-loan scheme's impact, demonstrating how Python analytics can measure socio-economic outcomes, financial sustainability, and inform better public policy decisions.

Analyzed a simulated micro-loan scheme using Python to assess repayment rates and socio-economic impact.

The program showed a strong 70% repayment rate and significantly increased beneficiaries' average daily income..

The 'Retail Trade' sector demonstrated the highest growth, indicating effective capital utilization.

Women, who made up 60% of recipients, were reliably high performers in both repayment and business growth.

The project demonstrates the dual success of achieving grassroots economic impact and financial sustainability.

Recommendations include implementing tiered loans for top performers and targeting high-impact sectors for scaling.
